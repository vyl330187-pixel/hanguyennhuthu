English plays an important role in todays globalized world. It is the most widely used language for international communication, education, science, and technology. Learning English helps students access a huge amount of knowledge from books, the internet, and modern media. Moreover, English improves career opportunities because many companies require employees to communicate with foreign partners. For students, mastering English also helps them study abroad and understand different cultures around the world. Therefore, English is not only a school subject but also an essential tool for personal development and future success.
